GPA Planner

  • This tool is only for students of 2018 Scheme and higher. 2017 Scheme students please refrain from using this tool.
  • This is GPA Planner which will give you approximate GPA to aim for each semester to score target GPA at the end of particular semester.
  • For example: You've to score 9 CGPA at the end of 6th semester and your first and second semester has passed and you've got 8.55 and 8.85 respectively. So, to know how much approximately you've to aim for in next 4 semesters to get 9 CGPA after 6th semester you can use this tool.
  • Correct way of typing SGPAs is "8.45,8.85,7.95" without quotes. Don't type like this "8.45, 8.85, 7.95".
