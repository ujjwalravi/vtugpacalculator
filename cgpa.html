CGPA Calculator Other Scheme

  • Enter the SGPA separated by just a comma (,). Make sure there is no space between two SGPAs except a comma.
  • This can calculate the CGPA for as number of SGPAs provided. For ex: You can calculate for 1st & 2nd sem or for 1st, 2nd & 3rd sem. Just make sure your given SGPAs starts from 1st sem and it can end at any sem.
  • Correct way of typing SGPAs is "8.45,8.85,7.95" without quotes. Don't type like this "8.45, 8.85, 7.95".
